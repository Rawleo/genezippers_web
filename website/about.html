The team behind the analysis of DNA compression efficiency.

Our Team

The "GeneZippers" project is a Senior Comprehensive Study (Comps) focused on evaluating and comparing the efficiency of DNA compression algorithms. Our goal was to determine how algorithms like DNAZip and Biocompress perform against standard compression methods when handling massive genomic datasets.
