From Bases to Bits:
An Evolution of DNA Compression Algorithms

This project evaluates three distincts data compression strategies to assess their efficacy in handling massive genomic datasets by comparing a general text-based approach (Huffman Coding) against two specialized DNA compressors: DNAzip (reference-based) and Biocompress 1 (non-reference-based).
